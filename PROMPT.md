# Generated Project Prompt

Build a polished MVP for a personal stock market news impact analysis system. The app should let a user choose a ticker, view a historical price and volume chart, overlay market news events, inspect event signals such as sentiment, event type, surprise level, volume anomaly, and post-event returns, and compare how similar past news influenced or correlated with the stock over 1-day, 3-day, 7-day, and 30-day windows.

Avoid claiming causation; describe results as associated market reaction. Use realistic sample data, a dense dashboard-style interface, and include clear setup/readme documentation.

## MVP Scope

- Static, zero-dependency frontend that runs in any modern browser.
- Ticker selector for a small sample universe.
- Interactive price and volume chart with news markers.
- News event list with sentiment, signal strength, and reaction windows.
- Detail panel explaining what happened around each event.
- Similar-event comparison based on event type and sentiment.
- Notes for future real data integrations.
